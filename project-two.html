Project 02

MissionZebra Collaboration

A team-driven project focused on helping families plan screen time in a healthier way. The product combines planning, structure, and motivating interactions to support children and parents in daily digital routines.

Team Project Mobile-first Real Users

Status

Live Product

  • Role Team Contributor
  • Stack Flutter, Kotlin
  • Focus Family workflow design

Impact Blocks

Family habits made practical

Shared Visibility

Parents and children can see progress in one place, turning conversations into clear agreements.

Positive Structure

Rewards and milestones transform limits into a motivating routine instead of a hard restriction.

Daily Momentum

Small repeated actions build long-term habits through consistency and predictable planning loops.

Execution Flow

How the team approached it

  1. Define realistic family use-cases and pain points around screen-time rules.
  2. Build a flow where goals, tracking, and rewards support each other naturally.
  3. Validate interaction clarity so the app feels helpful for both parents and kids.

Project Summary

What this project solves

MissionZebra makes screen-time habits actionable by turning abstract goals into concrete, trackable family routines. This creates structure and positive reinforcement instead of conflict.
