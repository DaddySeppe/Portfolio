# 🎬 QUICK START: Generate & Use Your Zoom Image

## What You Need (5 Minutes Total)

1. **Generate one image** (Midjourney or DALL·E)
2. **Save it** to `assets/images/zoom-base.jpg`
3. **Done!** Your scroll zoom is automatic

---

## The Easiest Prompt for Midjourney

Copy-paste this exactly:

```
    A professional developer sitting at a sleek dark desk with curved monitor glowing cyan code.
    Moody neon studio, hands on keyboard, sharp focus on monitor screen showing JavaScript syntax.
    Dark walls, professional photography, volumetric lighting, 8K, cinematic.
    --ar 16:9 --q 2
```

**Then:**
1. Open Midjourney (Discord)
2. Type `/imagine`
3. Paste the prompt
4. Wait 1 minute
5. Download the image
6. Save as `assets/images/zoom-base.jpg`
7. **Your site will instantly use it!**

---

## Where Your Website Zooms

When you **scroll down** on your portfolio:
- **0-15%**: Normal view
- **15-50%**: Monitor zooms in smooth 1x → 3x (WITH your image!)
- **50%+**: Zoom resets

The zoom effect is **already working** in your CSS/JavaScript. It just needs the image!
